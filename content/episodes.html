My Favourite Episode

"Admiral Peralta"

This is episode 10 of season 7 which is the 140th episode in the whole series. It was written by Neil Campbell and directed by Linda Mendoza (IMDb, 2020).

The episode starts with Jake and Amy announcing their pregnancy at the precinct. Their co-workers are not surprised though because Amy was not good at hiding it. The episode then follows the main plot where Jake invites his father, Roger, and estranged grandfather, Walter to the gender reveal party. Their bonding time then turns chaotic when Roger and Walter accidentally drop the gender reveal cake. This causes to the three men to try to clean up the cake blindfolded but that only makes things worse. The gender reveal ends up happening and more chaos ensues. Towards the end of episode Jake surprises Amy with a smaller cake, since she didn't know what the baby's gender is, and they find out that they're having a boy. (On the right is a clip from the episode).

This is my favourite episode because it is an amalgamation of all my favourite things about the show. It is funny, chaotic, it has some lighthearted moments, and it is centered around Peraltiago (Jake & Amy's couple name). No matter how many times I watch the show, this episode never gets old, and with each watch, I find something new to laugh at. In general, the episode was well-written and directed and the actors gave very believable performances which led to my ultimate enjoyment.
